The lab is happy to share that Grace Dykeman has received the L&S Honors Summer Research Apprenticeship which provides funding to allow UW-Madison honors students to gain meaningful research experience over the summer under the mentorship of a UW faculty member. Grace's project entails running independent analyses on data from an ongoing study to comprehensively examine the potential moderating role of gender in the relationship between cognitive reappraisal and internalizing symptoms.
